# Histogram + empirical CDF builder (roadmap #574)

Item 3 of the "next 5" roadmap-utilities batch. Adds the cumulative view of numeric samples, complementing the existing bin-counting histogram utils.

## Finish Report (2026-06-10)

**Scope:** (A) Dart library. New `lib/stats/cdf_utils.dart` (`CdfPoint`, `empiricalCdf`, `cdfAt`, `cumulativeHistogram`), new test, barrel export, CODE_INDEX row, CHANGELOG entry.

**Tests:** 7 cases ‚Äî duplicate collapse + probability accumulation to 1.0, unsorted-input handled without mutation, empty samples; `cdfAt` fraction at/below/above, empty‚Üí0; cumulative histogram running total, too-few-edges‚Üíempty. All pass; `flutter analyze` clean.
